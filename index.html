 Values

 Based on the Manifesto for Software Craftsmanship and Agile Software Development as well as our own empirical evidence.

Individuals & Interactions

Every human being is unique and thus may develop his own set of craftsman skills. We value self-organization, a sense of collaboration and open exchange.

Working Software

Functional Software is more valueable than producing other artifacts, like documents. The domain drives the value and enforces to quickly respond to changing requirements.

Code & Tools

Clean code does not exist without tests. A sign of true craftsmanship is to always pick the fitting tool set for the project. However: Focus on people, not tools

Continuous Improvement

Not only software, but also processes, tools, skills as well as individuals need and should always follow the path of constant improvement. Standstills are always steps backwards.

 Code of Conduct

 Based on the Code of Conduct of our Software Craftsmanship and KanDDDinsky as well as our own empirical evidence.

Long Version

Our events are dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or technology choices.

 

We do not tolerate harassment of participants in any form.

Participants violating these rules may be expelled from our events!

tl;dr

Be welcoming, friendly, and patient.
Be respectful.
Be aware of the effect your words may have on others.

 Event Host

Im Gegenzug freuen wir uns über eine tolle Location und evtl.  auch gesponserte Verpflegung, wie z.B. Getränke und Pizza!
